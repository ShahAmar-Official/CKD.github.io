Why ECG for CKD Detection?

ECG biosignals reveal systemic cardiovascular changes intrinsically linked to renal function. Our breakthrough research identifies subtle waveform patterns as early biomarkers of CKD progression, enabling non-invasive, real-time screening with unprecedented accuracy and scalability for global healthcare.
